Modern Elegance on a Premium Corner Lot

Welcome To 2147 Wateroak Drive — A Beautifully Appointed 2-Storey Detached Home Built In 2016 By Rockmount Homes In Northwest London. Situated On A Generous Corner Lot With 54 Feet Of Frontage, This Home Offers Exceptional Curb Appeal With Its Modern Brick And Stucco Facade.

Step Inside To A Grand Double-Height Foyer That Sets The Tone For The Rest Of The Home. The Main Floor Features A Stunning Open-Concept Layout With A Gourmet Kitchen Boasting A Large Island With Seating, Granite Countertops, White Cabinetry, Stainless Steel Appliances, And A Stone Backsplash — All Flowing Seamlessly Into The Spacious Living And Dining Areas Flooded With Natural Light Through Floor-To-Ceiling Windows.

Upstairs, The Luxurious Primary Suite Features Oversized Windows And A Spa-Like Ensuite With Dual Vanity. Two Additional Bedrooms Provide Ample Space For A Growing Family.

The Finished Basement Adds Valuable Living Space With A Recreation Room, Gym Area, And Additional Bedroom — Perfect For Entertaining Or Multi-Generational Living.

Additional Highlights Include A Fully Fenced Backyard With Large Concrete Patio, Attached Double Garage With Interlock Driveway, And A Quiet Family-Friendly Neighbourhood Close To Top-Rated Schools, Parks, Shopping, And Highway Access.
